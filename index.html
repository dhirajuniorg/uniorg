Features



Comfort

Comfort is one major factor as Biodyed fabrics are skin friendly and are comfortable to wear, due to lack of irritating dyes and toxic mordants the fabric is breathable which regulates body temperature and gives a cool feel as compared to synthetic dyed fabrics.

UV Protection

Dyed Yarn absorbs UV-light and the inherent, lifelong UV- protection Quality of Biodyes greatly reduces the scorching effect of direct sunlight making it ideal for beachwear and daily sun protection. Colours don't fade and bleed when washed with neutral soaps or dryed in the sun.

Odour lock

Due to special Ayurvedic properties Yarn dyed with Biodyes absorb and locks the odour of the body. The clean mordant used is Alum which is known to reduce body odour.

Colour fastness

Due to constant dedicated research and development on various colours, Biodyed products unlike other natural dyed products do not bleed or fade when washed or dryed in the sun. Infact under certain Coditions colours may get darker which increases UV protection.

About

Uniqorg is a private company owned by Young entrepreneurs and mechanical engineers based in Goa. Our company offers wide range of organic hand dyed comfort tshirts of high quality. Our core values are based on being completely natural and organic as well as being environmentally conscious. Our aim is to make people conscious about a better world in organic wear by using products which don't do any harm to the environment right from Cotton to the dyes we use.

Why should I buy from you and not from your competitor?

There are many brands who claim to provide natural dyed wear, but in reality they end up using semi-synthetic dyes and toxic mordants obtain their colours. Biodye offers a wide range of natural colourants of exceptionally high quality, the result of nine years of rigorous research and development. Dueto the absence of toxic metals , synthetic dyes and harmful chemicals, it is 100% natural. Biodye processes and materials adhere to recognized international organic standards and highest quality of dyeing is achieved. The dye house provides safe and fair trade conditions for employment of rural people and the waste products from dyeing of the yarn/fabric are non-polluting :the effluent is clear and can be released onto farmland while the solid waste can be composed to yield manure.
